About Premier Sushi

Our food cart was founded to provide an authentic Japanese sushi and hibachi experience that combines traditional techniques with a modern twist.

We use only the freshest ingredients and take great care in preparing each dish with precision and attention to detail.
Our menu features a variety of sushi rolls, burritos, bowls, burgers and donuts, and hibachi Japanese grill menus. Whether you're a sushi/hibachi lover or trying it for the first time, we have something for everyone. Our chefs are always experimenting with new flavors and ingredients to keep the menu exciting and fresh.
At our food cart, we believe that great food should be paired with excellent service. Our staff is knowledgeable, friendly, and always willing to go the extra mile to ensure that our customers have a wonderful dining experience.
We also offer catering services for events and special occasions. Whether you're planning a corporate event or a birthday party, we can provide delicious sushi platters and other Japanese specialties that will impress your guests.

Thank you for choosing us. We look forward to serving you soon!
